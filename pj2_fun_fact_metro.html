Metro Fun facts

In 1990 the movie “Total recall” by Arnold Schwarzenegger, used the Chabacano Metro station as a location for an action scene.

The Metro has a small vehicle or "baby metro" called dresina and is used to transport personnel who perform night work on the stations.

In addition, if you want to memorize all the metro stations, here you may find an interesting guide.

Also, the Metro has inspired a lot of songs, for example some music artist like Café Tacvba, Botellita de Jerez, El TRI, Los Estramboticos and Chava Flores has dedicated their inspiration to the Metro, each one of them with their own style.
